About Me

I am an enthusiastic and dedicated professional front end engineer with a keen eye for detail and determination to deliver the very highest quality. I'm currently working at Baker Hughes.

My passion and main areas of expertise are JavaScript, Angular, HTML and CSS, I'm focused on building products using JavaScript specifically Angular. JavaScript and frameworks based on it are of great interests to me, and I'm constantly on the lookout to learn or know more about the newest advancements in this sector. I take great pride in my work, and I always try to better myself with every project I work on.

Currently, I lead a group of front end developers at Baker Hughes, for one of their B2B eCommerce portfolio based on SAP Hybris. I use Spartacus - SAP's newest open source front end library based on Angular to create eCommerce websites for the various business entities in Baker Hughes.

When I'm not coding, I'm likely binge watching movies or following football.
